Hulda Thorruhn

A Frost Giant stands before you. Many a title follow her name. Hofgothi of the Shattered Flock. Mother, as seen by many. The Grand Seidr by those in need of Alchemy or Healing.

She shines with pure radiance. The calling of Amier echoes around her. Chanting of the fabled Valkyries can be heard within the grace of her holy light. A servant of the people, for the people through the Will of Amier.

"For it is because of Your sacrifice that we are blessed."
